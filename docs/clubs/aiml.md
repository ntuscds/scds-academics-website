# AI/ML Club

Welcome to the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI/ML) Club, a specialized community within SCDS focused on exploring and advancing AI and ML technologies.

## About the AI/ML Club

The AI/ML Club brings together students interested in artificial intelligence, machine learning, deep learning, and related fields. We create a supportive environment for learning, experimenting, and collaborating on AI/ML projects.

Our club caters to members of all skill levels, from beginners curious about AI to advanced practitioners working on research projects.

## Mission and Goals

- Foster interest and expertise in AI/ML among NUS computing students
- Provide resources and mentorship for learning AI/ML concepts and tools
- Create opportunities for hands-on AI/ML project experience
- Connect students with industry professionals and researchers in the field
- Promote responsible and ethical AI development practices

## Regular Activities

### Paper Reading Group

Every two weeks, we select and discuss influential research papers in AI/ML. This helps members stay current with the latest advancements while developing critical analysis skills.

### Technical Workshops

Regular hands-on sessions covering:
- Machine learning frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch)
- Computer vision techniques
- Natural language processing
- Reinforcement learning
- Data preprocessing and feature engineering

### Project Teams

Members form small teams to collaborate on AI/ML projects throughout the semester. Teams receive mentorship from experienced members and faculty advisors.

### AI/ML Competitions

We participate in competitions like:
- Kaggle competitions
- NUS AI Summit challenges
- International ML contests
- Industry-sponsored hackathons

### Industry Speaker Series

Monthly talks from professionals working in AI research and industry applications, providing insights into real-world AI implementation and career paths.

## Current Projects

Our members are currently working on several exciting projects:

- **NUSenti**: Sentiment analysis tool for student feedback
- **DeepCampus**: Computer vision system for campus navigation
- **MLforHealth**: Machine learning models for healthcare applications
- **AIGamers**: Reinforcement learning for game playing agents
