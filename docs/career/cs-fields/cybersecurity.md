# Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity focuses on protecting computer systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, unauthorized access, and information theft.

## Overview

Cybersecurity professionals are tasked with identifying vulnerabilities, implementing security measures, responding to incidents, and developing strategies to protect digital assets. This field continues to grow in importance as our world becomes increasingly connected.
